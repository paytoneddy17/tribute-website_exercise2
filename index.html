Tribute to Mrs. Sellers

Biography

Mrs. Sellers has shaped me into the person I am today. She is, without a doubt, the strongest, most selfless, kindhearted, and authentic person there is. Mrs. Courtney Sellers is the art teacher at Highland Middle School. During my time there, she was also one of the art teachers at Highland Elementary School. She would teach at the elementary school until lunch and then head over to the middle school to finish up the day. For most students, she was their rock, myself included. She has greatly improved the lives of everyone around her.

Achievements

  1. Started "Girl's Group" at Highland Middle School. A group where girls who are going through some things can come together to be heard and respected without judgement.
  2. Beat Colon Cancer! After her diagnosis in 2017, she beat "the beast" as she called it. This yeear marks 7 years cancer free.
  3. Has positively impacted every single person around her. There is not a day that goes by that I don't think about her. I don't even know where I'd be if she didn't push me into the best version of myself.

Payton Eddy - September 10th, 2024
